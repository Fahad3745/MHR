John Stevenson

CEO of Midshire Heritage Railway Hi my name is John and I am delighted to say that I am the CEO of such a great company. I believe that over the years we have vastly improved as a company and our branches have expanded over the years and also the number of employees have increased as many people start to see a career in our great company. One story I would like to share about myself is about how I had started this company from absolutely nothing, as I had completed university my future wasnt quite clear to me and unfortunately I struggled a lot because of this but one day a certain idea came to me I had been lent 50,000 Great British Pounds by my grandfather and he had always dreamt of openeing his own railway company and I told him that one day we would be successful and ensure we would create one of the best railway companies to ever exist and now here we are as one of the best railway companies it all started with a grandson fulfilling his grandfathers dreams.
